## Privacy Policy

Thermals does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information.

**What we store locally on your device:**
- Your pilot name
- Your high scores and altitude records
- Your sound preferences

This data never leaves your device and is removed if you uninstall the app.

**What we do NOT collect:**
- Your name, email, or contact information
- Location data
- Device identifiers or advertising IDs
- Analytics or crash reports
- Payment information

**Global Leaderboard**
Your chosen pilot name and scores are stored on secure servers to power the global leaderboard. No personal information is collected or linked to your identity.

**Advertising**
This app displays banner ads served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device information to serve relevant ads. See Google's privacy policy at policies.google.com/privacy

**Children's Privacy**
Thermals is suitable for all ages and does not collect any personal information from anyone including children under 13.
